History

Though not of the age old, it is historical to be talked of as an event springing from the times prior to the ones we now dwell in. The whole idea was conceived into the minds of the pioneers as a simple mathematical refreshment and stimulant, in form of a trivial internal quiz to mark the end of Maths year 2019. As a result, the last week of November, 2019 saw the Mathematicians of the band cruise through an oral problems based contest session in which a vigorous and spirited problem solving activity ensued, as new Mathematical ideas and elation permeated the psyches of the participants. It was the first of its kind at the time. This oral phase was then followed by a written presentation in the Arvo. The atmosphere of the day’s aftermath of the quiz then saw the Mathematicians resolve to be holding the kind of event annually. Hitherto, the Mathletics Contest still unfolds the glamour of Mathematics, year in, year out. After the Maths attack of the day(s), the club then crowns the distinguished Mathletes as well as an overall Maths cracker, the Maths King of the year.

About US

The K. C. Butiki mathematics Club is a dynamic and math bombarding group that is actively belaboring to sufficiently promote mathematics and create a ready platform that allows young people with passion in mathematics to share their creative ideas and algorithms to further advancement in the world of science through numbers. Full of mathematical activity due to our pure interest in maths, we are afire for anything numerical, lauching our mathematiical missels from Kiira college butiki, Jinja, Uganda, our base sealed with Mathematics my lifsetyle, the motto. The union is run by an executive committee of 15 maths combantants who are led by a President. The latter is answerable to an overall head of the union, the Patron; currently Mr. Mawanda Ismail (Since 2011). The Maths Club holds internal maths discussions for the local community, takes part in the National Mathematics Contest, organised by the Uganda mathematical Society; the Mathemania National Puzzel Contest that is organised by Galaxy and Horizon International Schools; the Mathletics Contest and organises online site blogs to popularize mathematics, and creating warm atmoshperes for maths lovers. Kept in action by our inhelent committements spelled out in the Motto, Vision and Mission statemets, our interest in never dimishing. Our Vision stands with this statement: “To always remain an avid and eminent exponent and promoter of mathematics to all zealous individuals and mathematicians.” We are day in day out hotly pursuing this vision through our mission directive, put forward in the mission statement: “To ensure provision and impartation of relevant and desired problem solving skills, promote excellence in external maths competitions and encourage innovation through mathematics.”

THE MATHEMATICS CONTEST

IT'S NATURE

The Mathletics Contest is an internal and annual sensational Maths competition of the Kiira College Butiki Mathematics Club, that brings together artistic and endowed lads of the hill to showcase their pre-eminent proficiency in Mathematics covering the four prominent areas of the IMO; number theory, combinatorics, geometry and algebra

The head panel of the contest accepts Mathletes below the age 20 and below, to take part in the contest. The contestants are divide into three categories namely Junior (Juvenile) category, Intermediate category and Senior category.

Learn More

JUNIOR CATEGORY

This level consists of Mathletes preferably below the age of 15. However, according to the country’s curriculum and school setting, a contestant that is 15 or above, but in Senior Two and below is accepted to take part under this category. The top Mathlete of this level is then chosen to compete in the Maths King Challenge platform.

Learn More

INTERMEDIATE CATEGORY

The intermediate category allows in contestants below the age of 17, though a Mathlete that is 17 or above who is in Senior 4 and below is allowed to compete in the Mathletics contest at the Intermediate platform. Likewise, the top Mathlete at the Intermediate level is also selected to go and compete at the Maths King Challenge platform.

Learn More

SENIOR CATEGORY

The oral part consists of short mind seizing and stimulating oral questions which are answered in time frame of a minute. The written phase consists of two sections A and B. In section A are 10 compulsory short answer stimulating problems from all the four areas of algebra, combinatorics, geometry and number theory in almost equal proportions. Each problem weighs exactly 5 points or marks. Section B has got exactly 5 high order mathematical problems each weighing 10 points or marks each, still from the four areas above in almost equal proportions. Problems vary according to category and therefore the level of their demands and appeal is proportional to the Mathletes’ levels
